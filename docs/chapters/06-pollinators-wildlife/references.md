# References: Pollinators, Wildlife, and Plant Relationships

1. [Pollination](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pollination) - Wikipedia - Explains pollination mechanisms including wind, insect, and bird pollination, describing how plants and pollinators have coevolved in mutually beneficial relationships.

2. [Monarch Butterfly](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Monarch_butterfly) - Wikipedia - Profiles the iconic Monarch butterfly and its dependence on milkweed plants, covering migration routes through Minnesota and conservation challenges it faces.

3. [Bee](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bee) - Wikipedia - Comprehensive overview of bee biology, diversity, and ecological importance as pollinators, including native bee species found throughout Minnesota's plant communities.

4. Tallamy, Douglas W. - Nature's Best Hope - Timber Press - Argues for creating connected habitat corridors using native plants to support pollinators and wildlife, with actionable steps for homeowners in any region.

5. Xerces Society - Attracting Native Pollinators - Storey Publishing - Practical guide to pollinator conservation covering native bee identification, habitat creation, and native plant selection to support diverse pollinator communities.

6. [Minnesota Pollinators](https://www.dnr.state.mn.us/pollinators/index.html) - Minnesota DNR - State resource on pollinator conservation in Minnesota, including species profiles, habitat recommendations, and the Governor's pollinator protection plan.

7. [Pollinator-Friendly Plants](https://extension.umn.edu/yard-and-garden-news/flowers-minnesota-bees) - University of Minnesota Extension - Research-based list of native plants that best support Minnesota's bee and butterfly populations, organized by bloom season to ensure continuous forage.

8. [Bee Lab at the University of Minnesota](https://beelab.umn.edu/) - University of Minnesota - Leading research program studying native and managed bee populations in Minnesota, offering educational resources on pollinator health and habitat needs.

9. [Pollinator Plants of Minnesota](https://www.minnesotawildflowers.info/) - MinnesotaWildflowers.info - Searchable database of Minnesota native plants filterable by pollinator attraction, helping gardeners select species that support bees, butterflies, and hummingbirds.

10. [Monarch Joint Venture](https://monarchjointventure.org/) - Monarch Joint Venture (University of Minnesota) - National partnership headquartered in Minnesota coordinating Monarch butterfly conservation, providing milkweed planting guides and citizen science opportunities.
