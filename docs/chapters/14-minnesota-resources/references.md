# References: Minnesota Native Plant Resources

## Wikipedia

1. [Minnesota Department of Natural Resources](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minnesota_Department_of_Natural_Resources) - Wikipedia - Describes the mission, history, and programs of the MN DNR, the primary state agency responsible for managing native plant communities and natural areas.

2. [University of Minnesota](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Minnesota) - Wikipedia - Covers the university's research programs, extension services, and role in agricultural and ecological science that directly support native plant conservation statewide.

3. [Conservation District](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Conservation_district) - Wikipedia - Explains the structure and purpose of soil and water conservation districts, which provide local technical assistance for native plantings and habitat restoration.

## Textbooks

4. Moyle, John B. and Evelyn W. Moyle. *Northland Wildflowers: The Comprehensive Guide to the Minnesota Region* - University of Minnesota Press - A definitive regional field guide covering identification, habitat, and range of wildflowers found throughout Minnesota's diverse ecological regions.

5. Smith, Welby R. *Trees and Shrubs of Minnesota* - University of Minnesota Press - Comprehensive reference for identifying native and naturalized woody plants in Minnesota, with distribution maps, habitat notes, and conservation status information.

## Online Resources

6. [Minnesota DNR Native Plant Communities](https://www.dnr.state.mn.us/npc/index.html) - Minnesota Department of Natural Resources - Official classification system for Minnesota's native plant communities, with detailed descriptions, species lists, and conservation rankings for each type.

7. [University of Minnesota Extension - Yard and Garden](https://extension.umn.edu/yard-and-garden) - University of Minnesota Extension - Provides research-based guidance on selecting, planting, and maintaining native plants suited to Minnesota's climate, soils, and growing conditions.

8. [Board of Water and Soil Resources - Native Vegetation](https://bwsr.state.mn.us/native-vegetation) - Minnesota BWSR - Supports local conservation efforts by providing native seed mix guidelines, pollinator habitat standards, and wetland restoration resources for Minnesota landowners.

9. [Minnesota Native Plant Society](https://mnnps.org/) - Minnesota Native Plant Society - A statewide organization that promotes conservation and appreciation of Minnesota's native flora through field trips, educational programs, and plant surveys.

10. [Restore Your Shore](https://www.dnr.state.mn.us/restoreyourshore/index.html) - Minnesota DNR - An interactive tool that helps Minnesota lakeshore owners select appropriate native plants for shoreline restoration based on site conditions and ecological goals.
