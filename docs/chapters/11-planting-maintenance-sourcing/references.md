# References: Planting, Maintenance, and Sourcing

1. [Native Plant](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Native_plant) - Wikipedia - Defines native plants and discusses their ecological roles, adaptations to local conditions, and importance for biodiversity conservation in regional landscapes.

2. [Seed Dormancy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seed_dormancy) - Wikipedia - Explains cold stratification and other dormancy-breaking mechanisms essential for successfully germinating native prairie and woodland plant seeds at home.

3. [Plug (horticulture)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plug_(horticulture)) - Wikipedia - Describes plug planting methods used in ecological restoration and native gardening, where small container-grown seedlings establish faster than direct seeding.

4. Native Plant Propagation Protocol - Matthew D. Albrecht and Ron Determann - University of Georgia Press - Technical reference covering seed collection, cleaning, stratification, and greenhouse growing methods for propagating native herbaceous and woody plant species.

5. The Know Maintenance Perennial Garden - Roy Diblik - Timber Press - Practical guide to designing and maintaining perennial gardens that thrive with minimal intervention, using plant community principles adapted from natural ecosystems.

6. [Prairie Moon Nursery](https://www.prairiemoon.com/) - Prairie Moon Nursery - Winona, Minnesota native plant and seed supplier offering over 600 species with detailed growing instructions, stratification guides, and seed mix calculators.

7. [Growing Native Plants](https://extension.umn.edu/planting-and-growing-guides/native-plants) - University of Minnesota Extension - Research-based guidance on selecting, planting, and caring for native plants in Minnesota, including soil preparation, watering schedules, and seasonal maintenance.

8. [Shooting Star Native Seeds](https://www.shootingstarnativeseed.com/) - Shooting Star Native Seeds - Spring Grove, Minnesota seed supplier specializing in locally sourced native seed mixes for prairie restoration, conservation plantings, and residential rain gardens.

9. [Minnesota Native Plant Sources](https://www.dnr.state.mn.us/gardens/nativeplants/suppliers.html) - Minnesota Department of Natural Resources - DNR directory of native plant nurseries and seed suppliers across Minnesota, helping gardeners source locally adapted genotypes for their projects.

10. [Seed Starting Native Plants](https://bwsr.state.mn.us/native-vegetation) - Minnesota Board of Water and Soil Resources - BWSR native vegetation establishment guides covering site preparation, seeding rates, planting timelines, and first-year maintenance for Minnesota conditions.
