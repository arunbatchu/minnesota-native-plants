# References: Capstone Projects

## Wikipedia

1. [Project-Based Learning](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Project-based_learning) - Wikipedia - Describes the educational approach where students gain knowledge by working on real-world projects, providing the pedagogical foundation for capstone experiences in ecology.

2. [Service-Learning](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Service-learning) - Wikipedia - Explains how community service integrates with academic study to deepen learning, a model well-suited to native plant restoration and conservation capstone projects.

3. [Portfolio Assessment](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electronic_portfolio) - Wikipedia - Covers how learners compile evidence of their work and reflect on growth over time, supporting the capstone portfolio approach used to document project outcomes.

## Textbooks

4. Boss, Suzie and Jane Krauss. *Reinventing Project-Based Learning* - International Society for Technology in Education - A practical guide for designing meaningful project-based experiences with strategies for student inquiry, collaboration, and authentic assessment in community contexts.

5. Eyler, Janet and Dwight E. Giles Jr. *Where's the Learning in Service-Learning?* - Jossey-Bass - A research-based examination of how service-learning produces educational outcomes, with evidence supporting reflection practices and community partnership models.

## Online Resources

6. [University of Minnesota Extension - Master Naturalist Program](https://extension.umn.edu/master-naturalist) - University of Minnesota Extension - Offers a volunteer naturalist certification program that models capstone-style community engagement projects focused on Minnesota natural resource education.

7. [Minnesota Conservation Volunteer](https://www.dnr.state.mn.us/mcvmagazine/index.html) - Minnesota DNR - Publishes articles on Minnesota conservation projects and citizen science opportunities that can inspire and inform student capstone project topics and approaches.

8. [Citizen Science Projects in Minnesota](https://www.dnr.state.mn.us/citizenscience/index.html) - Minnesota DNR - Lists active citizen science programs where students can contribute to real ecological data collection, providing authentic capstone project partnerships statewide.

9. [Buck Institute for Education - PBLWorks](https://www.pblworks.org/) - PBLWorks - Provides research-backed frameworks, rubrics, and project design tools that help structure high-quality capstone experiences with clear learning goals and assessment criteria.

10. [Minnesota GreenCorps](https://www.pca.state.mn.us/minnesota-greenCorps) - Minnesota Pollution Control Agency - An AmeriCorps program placing members with Minnesota organizations to work on environmental projects, offering a model for community-engaged capstone partnerships.
