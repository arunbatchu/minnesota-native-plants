# References: Prairie Plants and Grassland Ecosystems

1. [Tallgrass Prairie](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tallgrass_prairie) - Wikipedia - Describes the tallgrass prairie ecosystem that once covered western and southern Minnesota, including dominant grasses, wildflowers, and ecological processes.

2. [Prairie](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prairie) - Wikipedia - Broad overview of North American prairie types, their formation by fire and grazing, and the plant adaptations that enable survival in grassland environments.

3. [Echinacea](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Echinacea) - Wikipedia - Profiles the Purple Coneflower genus native to Minnesota prairies, covering species identification, ecological roles, and historical medicinal uses by Indigenous peoples.

4. Ladd, Doug and Oberle, Frank - Tallgrass Prairie Wildflowers - Falcon Guides - Field guide featuring color photographs and descriptions of prairie wildflowers found in the upper Midwest, organized by color for easy identification.

5. Helzer, Chris - The Ecology and Management of Prairies in the Central United States - University of Iowa Press - Comprehensive resource on prairie ecology, restoration techniques, and management through prescribed fire relevant to Minnesota's remnant grasslands.

6. [Prairie Restoration](https://www.dnr.state.mn.us/prairierestoration/index.html) - Minnesota DNR - State guidance on restoring native prairie in Minnesota, including seed mix recommendations, site preparation, and long-term management with prescribed burns.

7. [Prairie Plants of the Midwest](https://extension.umn.edu/yard-and-garden-news/planting-prairie) - University of Minnesota Extension - Practical advice on establishing prairie plantings in Minnesota, with species selection lists and maintenance schedules for home landscapes.

8. [Minnesota Prairie Plan](https://www.dnr.state.mn.us/prairieplan/index.html) - Minnesota DNR - Strategic conservation plan identifying priority prairie landscapes in Minnesota, documenting remaining native prairie and restoration opportunities.

9. [Prairie Identification Guide](https://www.minnesotawildflowers.info/grassland) - MinnesotaWildflowers.info - Photo database of grassland and prairie wildflowers found in Minnesota with detailed descriptions of habitat, bloom time, and distinguishing features.

10. [Tallgrass Prairie Center](https://tallgrassprairiecenter.org/) - University of Northern Iowa - Research and education center focused on prairie ecology and restoration, offering seed source guides and management resources applicable to the upper Midwest.
