# References: Ecological Restoration

1. [Ecological Restoration](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ecological_restoration) - Wikipedia - Comprehensive overview of the science and practice of assisting the recovery of degraded, damaged, or destroyed ecosystems through active human intervention and management.

2. [Tallgrass Prairie](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tallgrass_prairie) - Wikipedia - Describes the tallgrass prairie ecosystem historically covering western Minnesota, its plant composition, fire ecology, and ongoing efforts to restore remnant prairies.

3. [Wetland Restoration](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wetland_restoration) - Wikipedia - Explains methods for restoring degraded wetlands including hydrology repair, invasive species removal, and native plant reestablishment to recover ecosystem functions.

4. Principles of Ecological Restoration - Andre Clewell and James Aronson - Island Press - Foundational textbook presenting the theoretical framework and practical methods for restoring ecosystems, with emphasis on reference ecosystems and adaptive management.

5. The Tallgrass Restoration Handbook - Stephen Packard and Cornelia Mutel - Island Press - Essential field manual for planning and executing prairie and savanna restorations in the Midwest, covering seed harvesting, prescribed fire, and long-term monitoring.

6. [Prairie Restoration](https://www.dnr.state.mn.us/prairierestoration/index.html) - Minnesota Department of Natural Resources - Minnesota DNR guidance on restoring native prairies including site assessment, seed sourcing from local ecotypes, planting methods, and prescribed burn schedules.

7. [Wetland Restoration in Minnesota](https://bwsr.state.mn.us/wetlands) - Minnesota Board of Water and Soil Resources - BWSR wetland program providing technical standards, cost-share funding, and planning tools for restoring and protecting wetlands across Minnesota watersheds.

8. [Conservation Reserve Program](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/conservation-programs/conservation-reserve-program/) - USDA Farm Service Agency - Federal program providing financial incentives to Minnesota farmers who convert marginal cropland to native grassland, wetland, and forest cover for conservation.

9. [Society for Ecological Restoration](https://www.ser.org/) - SER - International professional organization setting standards and publishing guidelines for ecological restoration practice, including the widely referenced SER Primer on restoration.

10. [Minnesota Land Trust Conservation](https://www.mnland.org/) - Minnesota Land Trust - Statewide nonprofit working to permanently protect and restore ecologically significant land in Minnesota through conservation easements and restoration partnerships.
