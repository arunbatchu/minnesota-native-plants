# References: Plant Identification Skills

1. [Plant Identification](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plant_identification) - Wikipedia - Overview of methods used to determine plant species, including morphological features, habitat clues, and modern tools like DNA barcoding.

2. [Dichotomous Key](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dichotomous_key) - Wikipedia - Explains how dichotomous keys work as step-by-step tools for identifying organisms by choosing between paired characteristics at each decision point.

3. [Plant Morphology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plant_morphology) - Wikipedia - Covers the study of plant physical form and structure, including leaves, stems, roots, and flowers essential for field identification.

4. Newcomb's Wildflower Guide - Lawrence Newcomb - Little, Brown and Company - A uniquely organized field guide that uses a simple locator key based on flower type, plant type, and leaf type to identify wildflowers quickly.

5. Flora of Minnesota - Neil Moolenaar and Welby Smith - University of Minnesota Press - Comprehensive botanical reference covering vascular plant species found across Minnesota's diverse ecoregions with dichotomous keys and descriptions.

6. [Minnesota Wildflowers](https://www.minnesotawildflowers.info/) - Minnesota Wildflowers Info - Community-driven database with detailed photographs and descriptions of Minnesota wildflowers, grasses, and ferns organized by flower color and habitat.

7. [Plant Identification Resources](https://extension.umn.edu/planting-and-growing-guides/plant-identification) - University of Minnesota Extension - Extension guides for identifying common native and non-native plants in Minnesota landscapes, including trees, shrubs, and herbaceous species.

8. [Minnesota DNR Plant Field Guides](https://www.dnr.state.mn.us/wildflowers/index.html) - Minnesota Department of Natural Resources - State-maintained wildflower identification resource with photographs, habitat descriptions, and bloom times for species found across Minnesota.

9. [iNaturalist](https://www.inaturalist.org/) - iNaturalist / California Academy of Sciences - Citizen science platform using AI-assisted photo identification to help users identify plants and contribute observations to biodiversity databases.

10. [GoBotany](https://gobotany.nativeplanttrust.org/) - Native Plant Trust - Interactive online identification tool for New England and upper Midwest plants using simple questions about plant features to guide users to correct species.
