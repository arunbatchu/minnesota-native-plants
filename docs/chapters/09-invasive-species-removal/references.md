# References: Invasive Species Removal and Management

1. [Invasive Species Management](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Invasive_species#Management) - Wikipedia - Describes strategies for controlling invasive species including mechanical removal, chemical treatment, biological control, and integrated management approaches.

2. [Ecological Restoration](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ecological_restoration) - Wikipedia - Covers the science and practice of restoring degraded ecosystems after invasive species removal, including revegetation, soil recovery, and monitoring protocols.

3. [Herbicide](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Herbicide) - Wikipedia - Explains herbicide types, modes of action, and application methods relevant to controlling invasive plants while minimizing harm to native species and soil health.

4. Invasive Plant Management: Best Practices - Mandy Tu and Barry Rice - The Nature Conservancy - Practical field guide detailing cut-stump, basal bark, foliar spray, and prescribed burn techniques for removing invasive plants in natural areas.

5. Restoration Ecology: The New Frontier - Jelte van Andel and James Aronson - Wiley-Blackwell - Textbook covering ecological theory and field methods for restoring native plant communities on sites degraded by invasive species and land-use change.

6. [Buckthorn Removal Guide](https://www.dnr.state.mn.us/invasives/terrestrialplants/woody/buckthorn/index.html) - Minnesota Department of Natural Resources - Step-by-step instructions from Minnesota DNR for removing common and glossy buckthorn using mechanical and chemical methods tailored to Minnesota conditions.

7. [Managing Invasive Plants in Minnesota](https://bwsr.state.mn.us/invasive-species) - Minnesota Board of Water and Soil Resources - BWSR guidance on planning and implementing invasive species removal projects, including permit requirements, herbicide selection, and native seed sourcing.

8. [Invasive Species Best Management Practices](https://extension.umn.edu/landscape-design/managing-invasive-plants) - University of Minnesota Extension - Research-based recommendations for timing, tools, and follow-up care when removing invasive plants from residential landscapes and natural areas.

9. [Restore Your Shore](https://www.dnr.state.mn.us/restoreyourshore/index.html) - Minnesota DNR - Program helping Minnesota lakeshore owners remove invasive plants and restore native vegetation along shorelines to improve water quality and habitat.

10. [Midwest Invasive Plant Network](https://www.mipn.org/) - MIPN - Regional network providing invasive plant management guidance, volunteer coordination resources, and best management practice documents for the Upper Midwest.
