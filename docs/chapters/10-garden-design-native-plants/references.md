# References: Garden Design With Native Plants

1. [Native Plant Gardening](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Native_plant_gardening) - Wikipedia - Overview of designing gardens with indigenous plants to support local wildlife, reduce water use, and create low-maintenance landscapes adapted to regional conditions.

2. [Rain Garden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rain_garden) - Wikipedia - Explains rain garden design principles, including how planted depressions capture stormwater runoff, filter pollutants, and recharge groundwater using native plants.

3. [Pollinator Garden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pollinator_garden) - Wikipedia - Describes how gardens designed with native flowering plants provide essential nectar, pollen, and habitat for bees, butterflies, and other pollinating insects.

4. The Midwestern Native Garden - Charlotte Adelman and Bernard Schwartz - Ohio University Press - Design guide pairing native Midwest alternatives with common ornamental plants, showing gardeners how to create beautiful landscapes using regional native species.

5. Bringing Nature Home - Douglas Tallamy - Timber Press - Foundational book explaining why native plants are essential for supporting insect populations and food webs, with practical guidance for residential garden design.

6. [Blue Thumb Rain Garden Guide](https://bluethumb.org/rain-gardens/) - Blue Thumb / BWSR - Minnesota-focused resource for planning, building, and planting rain gardens with native species to manage stormwater and support pollinators in residential areas.

7. [Native Plant Garden Design](https://extension.umn.edu/landscape-design/landscaping-native-plants) - University of Minnesota Extension - Extension guidance on selecting, arranging, and maintaining native plants in Minnesota home landscapes, including species lists by sun and soil conditions.

8. [Prairie Moon Nursery Design Resources](https://www.prairiemoon.com/resources/) - Prairie Moon Nursery - Minnesota-based native plant nursery offering seed mix recommendations, planting guides, and garden design templates for prairie, woodland, and rain garden projects.

9. [Lawns to Legumes](https://bwsr.state.mn.us/l2l) - Minnesota Board of Water and Soil Resources - State program providing cost-share funding and technical guidance to help Minnesota residents convert lawns into pollinator-friendly native plant gardens.

10. [Xerces Society Pollinator Conservation](https://www.xerces.org/pollinator-conservation) - Xerces Society for Invertebrate Conservation - Habitat design guides and native plant lists for creating pollinator gardens that support bees, butterflies, and beneficial insects across the Upper Midwest.
