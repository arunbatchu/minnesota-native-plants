# Yarrow

*Achillea millefolium*



_No photos available yet. See [Learn More](#learn-more) links below._

Achillea millefolium, commonly known as yarrow () or common yarrow, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. Growing to 1 metre (3+1⁄3 feet) tall, it is characterized by small whitish flowers, a tall stem of fernlike leaves, and a pungent odor.
The plant is native to temperate regions of Eurasia and North America.
