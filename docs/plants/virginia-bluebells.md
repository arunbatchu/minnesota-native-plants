# Virginia Bluebells

*Mertensia virginica*

![White Bluebells (Mertensia virginica) growing in Central Illinois U.S.A.](img/virginia-bluebells-1.jpg)

![A bumblebee hanging from a bluebell in Pennsylvania, United States of America.](img/virginia-bluebells-2.jpg)

Mertensia virginica (common names Virginia bluebells, Virginia cowslip, lungwort oysterleaf, Roanoke bells) is a spring ephemeral plant in the Boraginaceae (borage) family with bell-shaped sky-blue flowers, native to eastern North America.
