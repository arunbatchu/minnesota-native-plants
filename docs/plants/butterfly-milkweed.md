# Butterfly Milkweed

*Asclepias tuberosa*

![Asclepias tuberosa, Asclepiadaceae, Butterfly Weed, fruits. The roots are used in homeopathy as remedy: Asclepias tubero](img/butterfly-milkweed-1.jpg)

![Species from central and eastern North America Common name: Butterfly Milkweed Photographed in the Camp Robinson Special](img/butterfly-milkweed-2.jpg)

Asclepias tuberosa, commonly known as butterfly weed, is a species of milkweed native to eastern and southwestern North America. It is commonly known as butterfly weed because of the butterflies that are attracted to the plant by its color and its copious production of nectar.
