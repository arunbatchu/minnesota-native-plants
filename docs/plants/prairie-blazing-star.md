# Prairie Blazing Star

*Liatris pycnostachya*



![Species from central North America Common name: Prairie Blazing Star Photographed along the Camp Robinson Special Use Ar](img/prairie-blazing-star-1.jpg)

![Self made scan of the seeds of Liatris pycnostachya, made 01/2008.](img/prairie-blazing-star-2.jpg)

Liatris pycnostachya, the prairie blazing star, cattail gayfeather, Kansas gayfeather, or cattail blazing star, is a perennial plant in the Asteraceae family that is native to the tallgrass prairies of the central United States.
