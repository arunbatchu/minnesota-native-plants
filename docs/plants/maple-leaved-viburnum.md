# Maple-leaved Viburnum

*Viburnum acerifolium*



![Range in North America via USDA](img/maple-leaved-viburnum-1.jpg)

![Photograph taken in Wrentham, MA on 19 October 2015 of a viburnum acerifolium (aka mapleleaf viburnum) bush. Viburnum ac](img/maple-leaved-viburnum-2.jpg)

Viburnum acerifolium, the mapleleaf viburnum, maple-leaved arrowwood or dockmackie, is a species of Viburnum native to eastern North America.
