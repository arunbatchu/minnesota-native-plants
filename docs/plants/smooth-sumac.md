# Smooth Sumac

*Rhus glabra*



![Smooth Sumac](img/smooth-sumac-1.jpg)

![Species from North America Common name: Smooth Sumac Photographed in Boyle Park, Little Rock, Pulaski County, Arkansas](img/smooth-sumac-2.jpg)

Rhus glabra, the smooth sumac (also known as white sumac, upland sumac, or scarlet sumac), is a North American species of sumac in the family Anacardiaceae.
