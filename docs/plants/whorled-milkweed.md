# Whorled Milkweed

*Asclepias verticillata*



![Species from central and eastern North America Common name: Whorled Milkweed Photographed in Gillam Park at the Little R](img/whorled-milkweed-1.jpg)

![Cluster of flowers on Asclepias verticillata (whorled milkweed). Taken 2013 Aug 18 in Pennsylvania, USA.](img/whorled-milkweed-2.jpg)

Asclepias verticillata, the whorled milkweed, eastern whorled milkweed, or horsetail milkweed, is a species of milkweed native to most of eastern North America and parts of western Canada and the United States.
