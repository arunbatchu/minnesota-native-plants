# Wild Onion

*Allium cernuum*



![The peduncle nods just below the flowering head, thus rendering the common name nodding onion.](img/wild-onion-1.jpg)

![Allium cernuum, Three Rivers Trail, Sierra Blanca, Lincoln County, New Mexico, 20 Jul 2010.](img/wild-onion-2.jpg)

Allium cernuum, known as nodding onion or lady's leek, is a perennial plant in the genus Allium. It grows in open areas in North America.
