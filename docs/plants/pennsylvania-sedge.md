# Pennsylvania Sedge

*Carex pensylvanica*



![A picture of Carex pensylvanica.](img/pennsylvania-sedge-1.jpg)

![Pennsylvania Sedge or Common Oak Sedge (Carex pensylvanica) in East Woods of the Morton Arboretum](img/pennsylvania-sedge-2.jpg)

Carex pensylvanica is a species of flowering plant in the sedge family commonly called Pennsylvania sedge (sometimes shortened to Penn sedge). Other common names include early sedge, common oak sedge, and yellow sedge.
